Step 1: Identify Where the Leak Appears

Take note of where water shows up inside the vehicle. The location of the drip often points directly to the source.

  • Above visor or A-pillar: Front roof panel seals
  • Over doors: Panel alignment or door frame seals
  • Rear cargo area: Rear hardtop or liftgate seal
  • Headliner seam: Panel-to-panel joint

Step 3: Inspect the Most Common Failure Points

Roof Panel Seals

Remove the roof panels and inspect the rubber seals for dirt, flattening, twists, or gaps at the corners.

  • Clean seals thoroughly
  • Allow to dry completely
  • Apply a light coat of silicone grease
  • Firmly reseat seals into their channels

Panel Alignment & Torque

Reinstall panels in order: front ‚Üí center ‚Üí rear. Tighten evenly‚Äîsnug, not cranked. Over-tightening causes distortion.

Final Verification

After repairs, let the vehicle sit overnight. Re-test with light water, then drive in real rain if possible. Recheck the interior the next morning.
