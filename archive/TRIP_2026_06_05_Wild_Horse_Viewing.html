Itinerary

  • June 5 (0900 hrs): Meet up in Lockwood NV (east of Reno on I-80). Travel to Virginia City and camp near Mt. Davidson that evening.
  • June 6: Head south to the Pine Nut Mountain wild horse range. Camping on BLM land within the range.
