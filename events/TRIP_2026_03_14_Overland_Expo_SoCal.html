March 14-15, 2026 | Costa Mesa, CA

The Overland East Bay crew is heading south for the premier overlanding event of the year! We're gauging interest for a group trip to Overland Expo SoCal and want YOU to join the adventure.

Why You Don't Want to Miss This

Overland Expo SoCal's inaugural 2025 event drew over 19,000 attendees, and 2026 promises to be even bigger. This is THE place where the entire overland community comes together for an unforgettable weekend.

What Makes This Expo Special:
  • 300+ Exhibitors showcasing the latest gear, vehicles, and innovations from leading overland and outdoor brands
  • 100+ Hands-On Classes covering everything from off-road driving techniques to vehicle recovery, camping skills, and expedition planning
  • New for 2026: Outdoor Lifestyle Pavilion featuring camping, surf, mountain biking, hiking, and photography gear—because overlanding is about what you do when you get there
  • The Overland Film Festival with inspiring travel stories and adventures from around the world
  • Exclusive Product Launches you'll see before anyone else
  • Ultimate Overland Vehicle Build showcase and DIY vehicle tours
  • Direct Access to Brands with opportunities to test gear, get expert advice, and snag show-exclusive deals

The Experience

Imagine walking through rows of custom-built rigs that'll make you rethink your entire setup. Attending expert-led training sessions that'll boost your confidence on the trail. Connecting with fellow overlanders who share your passion for adventure. And discovering game-changing gear you didn't even know existed.

The inaugural event saw many brands sell out of products on the first day—this is a serious gathering of outdoor enthusiasts ready to invest in their adventures.

Location: Costa Mesa

The OC Fair & Event Center in Costa Mesa, California provides the perfect Southern California backdrop. Plus, limited on-site camping is available if we want to make it a full weekend immersion with early access to vendors, after-hours community gatherings, and inspiring camp setups all around us.

Trip Details (To Be Determined)

  • Camp on-site at the expo for the full experience
  • Coordinate meeting points and group activities at the show
  • Plan optional off-road routes or coastal exploration before/after the event
