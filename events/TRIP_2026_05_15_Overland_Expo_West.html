Why You Don't Want to Miss This

Overland Expo West is the flagship event of the series. Set in the cool pine forests of Flagstaff, it draws over 30,000 attendees and 400+ exhibitors from around the world. It is the heart of the community.

What Makes This Expo Special:
  • The Global Gathering - Meet travelers who have driven around the world and see their legendary rigs in person.
  • 400+ Exhibitors - The largest collection of overlanding gear, campers, and vehicles anywhere.
  • Continuing Education - Hundreds of hours of classes, roundtables, and demos.
  • The Camel Trophy Field - Watch (or participate in) driving skills challenges.
  • The Vibe - There is nothing specifically like the energy of developing friendships around a campfire at West.

The Experience

Camping at Fort Tuthill puts you right in the action. Wake up to the smell of pine needles and coffee, spend the day learning and exploring, and end it with films, raffles, and stories by the fire. It's a high-altitude adventure festival.

Location: Fort Tuthill County Park

Located just south of Flagstaff, Fort Tuthill offers a stunning natural setting at 7,000 feet. The weather can be anything from sunny and warm to snowing, sometimes in the same day—so bring layers!
