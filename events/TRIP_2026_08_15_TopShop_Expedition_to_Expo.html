TopShop Expedition to Expo MNT-WEST

A journey along the Pony Express Trail to the Rockies

Some of us will join Frank from TopShop and his crew. He is planning a customer trip to the Overland Expo Mountain West in Loveland, Colorado.

Trip Details

  • The Route: We’re looking at a portion of the Pony Express Trail and descending the Rocky Backcountry Discovery Route (BDT) to Denver. The route crosses just east of Salt Lake City.
  • Timing: The week before the Expo. We will likely depart on August 15 or 16, arriving in time for the Expo which starts on the 21st.
  • The Destination: Overland Expo Mountain West in Loveland, Colorado.

This is a great opportunity to travel with experienced guides and a fun group!

Planning Phase

Note: We are currently in the early planning phase for this trip. Exact route details and meeting points will be refined as we get closer.

The Pony Express Trail

We will be following a portion of the historic Pony Express Trail. This legendary mail route operated from April 1860 to October 1861, connecting Missouri to California. It is a symbol of American West history, where riders galloped across the vast landscapes we will be exploring.

For more history and maps, visit the National Park Service - Pony Express Historic Trail.
