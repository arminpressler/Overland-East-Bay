Points of Interest

Kavanaugh Ridge

A spectacular high-alpine trail in the Eastern Sierra offering breathtaking views of Mono Lake and the surrounding peaks. Access involves rocky terrain and high elevation.

Bodie State Historic Park

A genuine California gold-mining ghost town. Visitors can walk down the deserted streets of a town that once had a population of nearly 10,000 people.

Ancient Bristlecone Pine Forest

Home to the oldest living trees on earth, some exceeding 4,000 years of age. A unique high-altitude landscape.

Mammoth Lakes

A stunning basecamp for adventure, known for its majestic mountains, crystal clear lakes, and endless outdoor opportunities.
