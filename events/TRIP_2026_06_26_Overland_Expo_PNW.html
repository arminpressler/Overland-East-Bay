Why You Don't Want to Miss This

Overland Expo PNW is known for its incredible vibe, set amongst the beautiful pine forests of Central Oregon. It is THE place where the entire overland community comes together for an unforgettable weekend.

What Makes This Expo Special:
  • 300+ Exhibitors showcasing the latest gear, vehicles, and innovations from leading overland and outdoor brands
  • 100+ Hands-On Classes covering everything from off-road driving techniques to vehicle recovery, camping skills, and expedition planning
  • The Overland Film Festival with inspiring travel stories and adventures from around the world
  • Exclusive Product Launches you'll see before anyone else
  • Ultimate Overland Vehicle Build showcase and DIY vehicle tours
  • Direct Access to Brands with opportunities to test gear, get expert advice, and snag show-exclusive deals

The Experience

Imagine walking through rows of custom-built rigs that'll make you rethink your entire setup. Attending expert-led training sessions that'll boost your confidence on the trail. Connecting with fellow overlanders who share your passion for adventure. And discovering game-changing gear you didn't even know existed.

Location: Redmond, OR

The Deschutes County Expo Center in Redmond, Oregon provides the perfect Pacific Northwest backdrop with the Cascades in the distance. Plus, camping is available on-site to make it a full weekend immersion with early access to vendors, after-hours community gatherings, and inspiring camp setups all around us.
