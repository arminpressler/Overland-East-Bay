Monthly Meetup

Overland East Bay • Every 2nd Sunday

Join us for our regular Second Sunday meetup! It really doesn’t matter what you drive just come ready for adventure. Each month we talk about overlanding, plan new trips, and discuss various aspects of vehicle-based travel.

No need to signup - just show up.
